📖 Reading Comprehension for Beginners (Grades 4-8)

Grammar Vocabulary Comprehension

📝 Reading Passages

Read the passages below and answer the questions to improve your comprehension skills.

🌟 Tips for Better Comprehension

  • Read the passage carefully and try to understand the main idea.
  • Look for key details that support the main idea.
  • Try reading aloud to improve your understanding.
  • Ask yourself: Who? What? When? Where? Why?

📜 Passage 1: The Curious Cat

Once upon a time, a small cat named Whiskers was very curious about everything. He would peek into every corner, climb on furniture, and explore the garden daily. One day, he saw a butterfly fluttering around and decided to chase it...

Question: What did Whiskers love to do?

📜 Passage 2: The Busy Ants

In a tiny hole beneath a tall tree, a colony of ants worked tirelessly. They carried food pieces much larger than themselves back to their nest. Among them was a small ant named Andy, who had the job of finding the best food for the queen...

Question: What was Andy’s job?

🎯 Fun Activities

  • Highlight the important words in each passage.
  • Write a short summary of the story in your own words.
  • Draw a picture based on the story and share with your class.
