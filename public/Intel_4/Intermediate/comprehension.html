The Mysterious Cave

One summer afternoon, Alex and Mia were hiking near their town when they discovered a hidden cave behind a waterfall. Curious, they decided to explore it. As they entered, they found strange symbols on the walls. "Maybe it's an ancient language!" Mia exclaimed. Alex took out his notebook and started sketching the symbols. Suddenly, they heard a soft echoing sound. "Did you hear that?" Alex whispered. Their adventure had just begun.

As they moved further inside, they found a small chest covered in dust. They carefully opened it and found a letter that read, "To those who seek knowledge, wisdom is your greatest treasure." Excited by their discovery, they decided to research the symbols when they returned home.
