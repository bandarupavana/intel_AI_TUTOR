1. Synonyms and Antonyms

Improve your word power by learning synonyms (words with similar meanings) and antonyms (words with opposite meanings).

  • Happy - Synonym: Joyful, Cheerful | Antonym: Sad, Miserable
  • Brave - Synonym: Courageous, Fearless | Antonym: Cowardly, Timid
  • Fast - Synonym: Quick, Speedy | Antonym: Slow, Sluggish

2. Commonly Confused Words

These words sound alike but have different meanings:

  • There (a place) vs. Their (belonging to them) vs. They're (they are)
  • Two (number) vs. Too (also) vs. To (direction)
  • Effect (result) vs. Affect (to influence)

3. Idioms and Expressions

Idioms are phrases with meanings different from their literal words.

  • Break the ice - To start a conversation in a social setting.
  • A piece of cake - Something very easy to do.
  • Hit the sack - To go to bed.
