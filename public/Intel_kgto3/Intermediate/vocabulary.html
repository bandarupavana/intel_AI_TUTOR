1. Opposites (Antonyms)

Some words mean the opposite of each other. Let's learn them!

2. Words That Sound the Same

Some words sound the same but have different meanings.

  • See (to look) vs. Sea (big water)
  • Sun (in the sky) vs. Son (a boy in a family)
  • Blue (a color) vs. Blew (past of "blow")
