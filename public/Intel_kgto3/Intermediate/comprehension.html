📚 Read the Story and Answer the Questions

The Lost Kitten

One sunny morning, Lily was playing in the garden when she heard a soft "meow." She looked around and saw a small kitten under a bush. The kitten looked hungry and scared. Lily gently picked it up and took it home.

She gave the kitten some milk and wrapped it in a warm towel. "Don’t worry, little one," she said. After some time, the kitten felt safe and started purring. Lily’s mom helped her find the kitten’s owner. The neighbor, Mrs. Green, was so happy to see her lost kitten again!

📝 Questions

  • 1. Where did Lily find the kitten?
  • 2. How did the kitten feel when Lily found it?
  • 3. What did Lily do to help the kitten?
  • 4. Who was the kitten’s owner?
  • 5. How do you think Lily felt when she returned the kitten?
