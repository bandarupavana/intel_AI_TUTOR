🦉 The Secret Treehouse

One sunny morning, Emma and Noah were playing in the forest near their house when they noticed something unusual. Behind the tall oak tree, hidden under thick leaves, was a tiny wooden door! "Where do you think it leads?" Noah asked.

Carefully, they pushed the door open. Inside, they found a magical treehouse filled with books, sparkling fairy lights, and a talking owl! "Welcome, young explorers!" the owl hooted. "Only those who love stories can enter this special place."

Emma and Noah looked around in amazement. On the table was an old treasure map! The map showed a path leading to a **golden key** buried near the biggest tree in the forest. "Should we go on an adventure?" Emma asked excitedly.

Just as they were about to leave, the owl spoke again. "Remember, kindness and bravery will always help you find the way!" Their adventure was just beginning...

📝 Comprehension Questions

  • 1. Where did Emma and Noah find the tiny door?
  • 2. What was inside the secret treehouse?
  • 3. What did the treasure map show?
  • 4. What advice did the owl give them?
  • 5. If you found a secret treehouse, what would you do?

📖 Vocabulary Words

  • unusual - something different or special
  • amazed - very surprised
  • bravery - being strong and not afraid
  • sparkling - shining or twinkling
  • adventure - an exciting journey

✍️ Creative Writing Activity

Imagine you found the golden key from the story. What does it open? Write a short story about what happens next!
