📖 Learn New Words (KG - Grade 3)

Grammar Vocabulary Comprehension

📝 Learn New Words!

Let’s have fun learning words that help us read, write, and talk better!

🌟 Word Learning Tips

  • Say new words out loud and use them in sentences.
  • Look at pictures in books to learn new words.
  • Play word games with family and friends.

📌 Naming Words (Nouns)

Nouns are words that name people, places, and things.

Examples: girl, dog, school, apple

📌 Action Words (Verbs)

Verbs are words that tell what someone or something does.

Examples: run, jump, eat, sing

📌 Describing Words (Adjectives)

Adjectives are words that tell us more about a noun.

Examples: big, red, happy, soft

📌 How Words (Adverbs)

Adverbs tell how something happens.

Examples: quickly, loudly, slowly

🎲 Fun Practice

📝 Match the Words!

Match the words to the right group:

1. The soft pillow. (Noun, Verb, Adjective?)

2. The cat jumps. (Noun, Verb, Adverb?)

3. She walks quickly. (Adjective, Adverb, Noun?)

4. The dog barks. (Noun, Verb, Adjective?)

🎯 Let’s Play a Game!

  • Find five things in your room and say their names (Nouns).
  • Jump, clap, or spin and say what you did (Verbs).
  • Look around and say a color or size of something (Adjectives).
  • Walk fast or slow and describe how you moved (Adverbs).
