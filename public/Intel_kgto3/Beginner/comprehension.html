📖 Fun Reading for Little Learners (KG-3)

Grammar Vocabulary Stories

📝 Fun Stories

Read these short stories and answer simple questions.

🌟 Tips for Good Reading

  • Look at the pictures and think about what the story is about.
  • Read slowly and say each word clearly.
  • Ask yourself: Who is in the story? What happens?
  • Try telling the story to a friend or family member.

📜 Story 1: The Playful Kitten

There was a little kitten named Mimi. She loved to chase butterflies and play with her ball. One day, she found a big red ball and rolled it around the yard...

Question: What did Mimi love to do?

📜 Story 2: The Hardworking Ants

Under a big tree, some tiny ants were very busy. They carried food back to their home. One little ant named Andy found a big cookie crumb and pulled it all the way to the nest...

Question: What did Andy find?

🎯 Fun Activities

  • Circle the important words in each story.
  • Draw a picture of what happens in the story.
  • Tell the story to a family member using your own words.
