📝 Learn Words

Let's explore words and how we use them.

🌟 Grammar Basics

  • Sentences need a person or thing and an action.
  • Describing words make sentences fun.
  • Action words tell what happens.
  • Try making short sentences!

📌 Naming Words (Nouns)

Names of people, places, or things.

Examples: Mom, School, Dog, Ball

📌 Action Words (Verbs)

Tell what someone or something does.

Examples: Jump, Eat, Sleep, Play

📌 Describing Words (Adjectives)

Tell more about naming words.

Examples: Happy, Big, Red, Soft

📖 Sentences

📌 Short Sentences

Have a naming word and an action word.

Example: The cat runs.

📌 Joining Sentences

Use words like 'and' to connect sentences.

Example: The cat runs, and the dog barks.

🎯 Fun Activities

  • Write three short sentences.
  • Find action words in a picture book.
  • Pick a toy and describe it with three words.
