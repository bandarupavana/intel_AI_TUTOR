📖 Fun Words for Kids (KG - Grade 3)

Grammar Vocabulary Comprehension

📝 Learn New Words

Let's learn some fun words! Words help us talk, read, and write better. 😊

🌟 Word Fun Tips

  • Look at pictures and say the words out loud.
  • Sing a song with new words.
  • Play a game where you find things around you and say their names.

📌 Naming Words (Nouns)

Nouns are names of things, places, and people.

Examples: Dog, Car, Mom, School

📌 Action Words (Verbs)

Verbs tell what we do.

Examples: Jump, Run, Eat, Dance

📌 Describing Words (Adjectives)

Adjectives tell us more about things.

Examples: Big, Happy, Red, Soft

🎲 Let's Play!

📝 Match the Word

Can you match the word with its picture? Ask your teacher or parent for help!

  • 🐶 Dog - (Noun)
  • 🏃 Run - (Verb)
  • 🎈 Red - (Adjective)

🎯 Fun Activities

  • Find 3 things in your room and say their names (nouns).
  • Act out an action (jump, clap, run) and ask someone to guess it!
  • Point to something and say its color or size (red, big, soft).
