📌 Grammar Basics

✅ Subject-Verb Agreement

The verb must agree with the subject in number (singular/plural).

Example: He goes to school. (✔)
They go to school. (✔)

✅ Articles (a, an, the)

Use "a" before consonant sounds and "an" before vowel sounds.

Example: I saw a cat.
She ate an apple.

✅ Simple Present vs. Present Continuous

Use Simple Present for daily routines, and Present Continuous for ongoing actions.

Example: She reads every night. (Daily habit)
She is reading now. (Ongoing action)
