📝 Vocabulary Basics

📌 Nouns

Nouns are words that name people, places, or things.

Examples: Apple, Dog, School, Happiness

📌 Verbs

Verbs describe actions.

Examples: Run, Jump, Read, Write

📌 Adjectives

Adjectives describe nouns.

Examples: Beautiful, Tall, Fast, Strong
