📚 Reading Comprehension

📝 Read the Passage:

Emma loves books. Every evening, she sits by the window with her favorite novel. One day, she found an old book in the library that had a note inside. It was a message from a student who read it years ago! Emma was excited to learn about the student’s thoughts on the book.

🔍 Answer These Questions:

1. Where does Emma sit while reading?

2. What did she find in the book?
